How much will it cost?
Every cleaner on Hassle.com charges a flat rate of £10 per hour. A refundable holding deposit of £10 is all that is required to secure your booking and this is taken off the cost of your first clean. If we can't find you a great cleaner, we'll refund you the money straight away, no questions asked.

How do I pay?
Payment will be taken from your credit / debit card once the clean is complete. This means you never have the hassle of remembering to have to get cash and it’s all processed safely and securely.

Can I cancel?
We're confident that you will fall in love with Hassle.com but you are free to cancel at any point up to 24 hours before your next booking. If you cancel with less than 24 hours to go there is a £15 cancellation charge.
